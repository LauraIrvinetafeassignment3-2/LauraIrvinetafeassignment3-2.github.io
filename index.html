Garden's Fruit and Vegetables

The Freshest Food Delivered To Your Door

Garden’s Fruit and Veg is a traditional store selling fruit and vegetables, along with other commodity items such as milk, soft drinks, sweets etc. Garden’s has been in business for 27 years and has a reputation for the quality of their goods. The proprietor, Mike Garden, has discovered that there are many people who, through work schedules and home lifestyles, don’t have the opportunity to shop in person. For convenience, they do their fruit and vegetable shopping at supermarkets, but are not happy with the quality of the produce from the supermarkets. Mike wants to tempt these customers back by taking his store to them, in their homes.
